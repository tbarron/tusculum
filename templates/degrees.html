[Degree [Minute [Second]]] Converter

This page will convert fractional degree values (eg., 36.75342) to degree + fractional minutes (eg., 36 45.2052) or to degree + minute + fractional seconds (eg., 36 45 12.312).

Fill in whichever form of the coordinate you have and click the "calculate" button on the right to compute and fill in the other forms of the value.

Note that only one coordinate (latitude or longitude) is handled at a time.
