Calories per Pound

Enter calories per serving, servings per package, and ounces (or grams) per package,

OR just enter calories per ounce (or gram),

then press Calculate to compute calories per pound.

Pressing Clear will reset the form.

To compute calories per gram, enter the first three values, select 'Gram' in the Calories per Ounce/Gram selection list, and press Calculate.
