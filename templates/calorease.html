Calories per Pound

  • Enter calories per serving, servings per package, and ounces (or grams) per package (the pink fields),

  • OR just enter calories per ounce or gram (the blue field) ,

  • then press Calculate to compute calories per pound (the lavender field).

  • Pressing Clear will reset the form.

  • If you have calories per ounce (or gram), and want calories per pound, leave the first three (pink) fields blank, select the desired weight unit in the calories per ounce/gram selection list, enter the number of calories per unit weight, and press Calculate.
